How this works & FAQ

What is this?

A web version of open-razer-py. It talks to your Razer device directly over USB using the browser's WebHID API and speaks Razer's own lighting protocol — the same bytes the desktop tool sends. No Synapse, no drivers, no account.

Is it safe / private?

Yes. Everything runs in your browser on your machine. Nothing is uploaded; there's no server. The page can only reach the one device you explicitly pick, and only while the tab is open.

What does “save to device” do?

Razer devices have a little onboard memory. With save on, your color is written there, so it sticks after you unplug or reboot — with nothing running. Your last color is also remembered in this browser.

My color didn't change / I get an error

Two common causes: Synapse or another RGB app is open (close it — it keeps re-applying its profile), or Chrome blocks WebHID on a device whose control lives on a “protected” interface — those need the CLI.
